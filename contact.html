Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are your delivery options?

    We offer standard delivery (3-5 business days) and express delivery (1-2 business days) across Pakistan. Delivery charges vary based on your location and the size of your order.

  • What is your return policy?

    We offer a 14-day return policy for most items in original condition with packaging. Some items like software, accessories, and personalized products may have different return conditions.

  • Do you offer technical support?

    Yes, we provide technical support for products purchased from us. You can contact our support team via phone, email, or visit our service center during business hours.

  • How can I track my order?

    Once your order is shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email and SMS. You can use this tracking number on our website or the courier company's website to track your order.

  • Do you have a physical store?

    Yes, we have a physical store at 123 Tech Street, Gulberg III, Lahore. You can visit us during business hours to see products in person and make purchases.
